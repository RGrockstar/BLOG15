St. Xavier school

St. Xavier school is a Central board of Secondary education

St. Xavier's School, India, is a Jesuit, Catholic, co-educational, prep school founded in 1960. It is located in Raj Niwas Marg in the Civil Lines area of North Delhi (India). The Jesuits (Society of Jesus) are an international, Catholic-Christian religious order founded by St. Ignatius of Loyola in 1540. The school is recognised by the Directorate of Education, Delhi Administration, and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education for the All India Secondary School Examination and All India Senior School Certificate Examination.[1] An affiliated school in nearby Rohini includes a Junior School, Vocational Training Institute, and a National Institute of Open Schooling (afternoon tutorial). This school occupied its new site on 1 May 1998 and has enjoyed the administrative assistance of the Congregation of Jesus (CJ) sisters since 2006.

Campus Life

School library

Was established in 2010

Student’s read books from different categories including fiction, non-fiction, biography, autobiography, fantasy, novels, narratives, science fiction, historical fiction, poetry and many more. All of these are segregated into different sections in the libraries. There are two libraries which are primary and secondary.

School cafeteria

Was established in 2010

The cafeteria is not only for food but for open stage performances and whole school assemblies. There is a stage in front of the first side of sitting which is for the assemblies and performances. When there are such events, the dining is transformed into sitting area. There is a salad bar for pickles, salad, lemons, chilies and more. The normal food includes at least one sweet, three to four types of curries and a foreign food like pasta or noodles.

School achievements

Sports day
Medals are given to each winner.

Sport’s Day happens in February every academic year where different events and showcases take place. There are horse riding performances, cheerleading dance by students, inter-house competitions of each sport available at school, sports run and many more things. Medals are given to each winner.

Inter house football competition
Medals or Trophies are given to winners in the whole school assembly coming up after the competition is over.

Inter-house football competitions are held frequently in school where there are house teams made and all rules set have to be followed. Children play football like normal and there is a color coded coin spinned to choose the teams amongst the four houses. After that, two-two teams each play against each other. This will inculcate all the four houses all at once. Lastly, the teams that won will have a match together to find the first and second place. Similarly, the teams that lost will have a match together to figure out the third and fourth place. Medals or Trophies are given to winners in the whole school assembly coming up after the competition is over. This competition takes place in the P.E. lesson for each grade.
